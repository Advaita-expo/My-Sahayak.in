Who We Are

"Dignity begins with opportunity."

We are a social enterprise dedicated to empowering men and women from marginalised communities.

At My Sahayak, we provide skills training, professional development, and dignified employment as trusted home staff.

Our goal is twofold — to uplift lives through meaningful work and to bring reliability and care into every home. Each member of our workforce undergoes rigorous training, thorough background verification, and strict reference checks.

With My Sahayak, you don't just hire help — you support a mission that transforms lives.
