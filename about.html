About My Sahayak

Our Mission

Dignity Begins with Opportunity

At My Sahayak, we believe that every individual deserves the chance to build a better life through meaningful work. We are a social enterprise dedicated to empowering men and women from marginalized communities.

We provide skills training, professional development, and dignified employment as trusted home staff. Our goal is twofold — to uplift lives through meaningful work and to bring reliability and care into every home.

"With My Sahayak, you don't just hire help — you support a mission that transforms lives."
